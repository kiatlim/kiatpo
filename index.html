Kiat's Bio

I've been designing & building websites for over a decade.

In 2011, I co-founded Pixel Onion, a Drupal specialist firm that strives to empower organisations to take control of their web presence. We work closely with you to build websites that work well, look great and are easy to manage.

On the side, I volunteer at IxDA Singapore & Drupal Developer Network Singapore. These are communities where people enjoy a good evening of passionate conversation about UX & Drupal. Occasionally, you may spot me share in a Drupal related events such as Drupal meetup or DrupalCamp.

If you're passionate about making the web a better experience for everyone, let's connect!
